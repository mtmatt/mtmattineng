Composition Practice : A Trip

2021-07-17
CompositionPractice
taiwan

This was in a summer vacation, in 2010. We were going to the Janhusun Fancyworld. A fancy world lying in Yunlin. Chatting in the car, we arrived. It was a special fancy world with two part, land world and water world. We can’t go into water world because we hadn’t bought the ticket. We had a nice day until my brother’s phone lost in the Janhusun Fancyworld. We went fast to find his phone. Finally, we found his phone, and we were exhausted when we were in the hotel.
That’s why I don’t like to go to fancy world as a trip. I found that when anyone goes to a fancy world for a trip, they will always be exhausted and upset. But some people will feel boring when the trip without exciting things, I think maybe we should find a balance between excited and relax, or we will feel either bored or upset.

This story is not true.
